## Sensor Network Architecture

### Physical Deployment

The 203 sensors are strategically placed throughout the data center to provide comprehensive temperature monitoring:

- **Cold Aisles**: 68 sensors monitoring supply air temperature
- **Hot Aisles**: 75 sensors tracking exhaust temperatures  
- **Equipment Zones**: 45 sensors near critical infrastructure
- **Ambient Areas**: 15 sensors for baseline measurements
